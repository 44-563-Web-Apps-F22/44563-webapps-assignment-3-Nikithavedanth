Salarjung Museum

There are Persian carpets, scriptures and glassware Paintingsfrom England, Ireland, France, Belgium, Italy, Germany etc. would surely awe the visitors. There is also a painting of a township which changes its perspective with respect to the viewer's point. The exquisite detailing of the carvings made on ivory would surely enchant the visitor. There are exhibits from the collection of the Mughal Emperors.

Airport closest to the Museum

The airport closest to the museum is Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

    1. We first take right exit to reach mehdipatnam.
    2. Then from there take left to go to hussain sagar.
    3. Go straight and then right to himayath nagar.
    4. From there take 2nd exit to afzal ganj.
    5. Go straight you will reach Dar-ul-Shifa and that is your destination.
